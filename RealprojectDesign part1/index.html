About Us

We are the newest destination for quality food and drink offerings in Sydney’s inner west and we are already being named as the area’s new favourite local. Extensive renovations on the spacious ground floor area have included the introduction of The Burwood Eating House, a casual dining venue with an ‘East meets West’ style food and cocktail menu.

If you’re more of a night owl, we are a fantastic spot for some midnight dining on the way home from the CBD - with the kitchen open from 11.30am to 2am Thursday through Saturday nights, and until 10pm Sunday through Wednesday.
